🎯 How to Score High in 10th Class

Preparing for board exams can be stressful, but with the right approach, you can achieve excellent results without unnecessary pressure. At Study Point, we follow a step-by-step strategy to help students consistently score high in their 10th board exams.

  • Step 1: Master Your NCERT 📘 – We ensure that students study every chapter of their NCERT books thoroughly — not just the exercises, but the solved examples as well.
  • Step 2: Chapter-wise Testing 📝 – After completing a chapter, we conduct a dedicated test for it. Long chapters are split into two to make it manageable.
  • Step 3: Extra Questions Practice 📗 – We move on to trusted books like RD Sharma, RS Aggarwal for extra practice.
  • Step 4: Reference Book Tests 🧪 – Tests based on extra books are conducted to reinforce learning.
  • Step 5: Model Test Papers 📄 – Practice with model test papers from Oswal to strengthen time management skills.
  • Step 6: Mock Exams 🎓 – We conduct mock board tests using sample and previous year papers to simulate real exam environment.

“Practice is the key to success.”

📌 What Stream Should You Choose After 10th?

Choosing the right stream after Class 10 can shape your future. At Study Point, we understand how confusing this phase can be, so we help students and parents with proper guidance and counselling.

  • Step 1: It’s Not Only About Interest 🤔 – The famous speaker Acharya Prashant, whom I deeply follow, says interest can be developed in anything if done with the right intent (niyat). So, think wisely about which subjects you're actually strong in.
  • Step 2: Dedication Matters 💯 – Ask yourself: Which subject can you give your 100% to? If you have scored very low in science or maths, don’t force yourself into it. Challenges are good, but not at the cost of your peace. 🚫
  • Step 3: Stream Determines Future 🧭 – Your career path begins with the stream you choose. It’s better to choose based on your subject results than to regret later. 🔍

⚠️ Warning Sign for Parents:

Don’t pressure your child to fulfill your dreams. If your child is weak in one subject, it doesn’t mean they are weak overall. Every student is good at something — let them discover and follow that. 🌱💡

📘 Crack Class 12 Board Exams with Confidence

Preparing for Class 12 board exams can feel overwhelming — but with the right mindset and consistent effort, you can master any subject! At Study Point, we’ve designed a focused strategy specifically for Class 12 students to maximize their performance. 🚀📚

  • Step 1: Understand the Syllabus 📖 – Know what's important. Focus on chapters that carry more weight in the exam.
  • Step 2: Practice Previous Year Papers 📄 – Solving past 5 years’ questions helps you understand the pattern and question types.
  • Step 3: Learn Conceptually 🧠 – Don’t just memorize. Understand the ‘why’ behind every topic for subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Economics.
  • Step 4: Make Short Notes ✍️ – For quick revision, write down formulas, key points, and definitions in one place.
  • Step 5: Revise Smartly ⏳ – Revise in weekly cycles using mind maps, flashcards, or peer quizzes.

🌟 Motivation: You don’t need to study 10 hours a day — just make your 4 hours count! Quality > Quantity.
