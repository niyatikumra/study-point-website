Our Founder

The founder of Study Point Mrs. Babita Kumra has more than 25 years of teaching experience. She has degrees of B.Com, M.Com, and B.Ed. She specializes in teaching Maths and Accounts from Class 9 to 12, maintaining a 100% pass percentage. She understands student psychology very well. With her guidance and support, many students have shaped their lives better through improved results and better career guidance. Some have become great CAs, others have excelled in the banking sector, and some in teaching.

Our Belief

The first thing we believe in is that “a teacher can only be a great teacher when not only toppers but even backbenchers get the best results.” Our key is taking tests not only during the week but every Sunday as well, as we believe practice makes a human perfect.

With years of experience in teaching, our center is not like other famous centers that fill large numbers of students in the same class. We believe in student–teacher interaction more than money. There are fixed number of students in every batch – a maximum of 20 – so that each teacher can give attention to every student.

We have qualified teachers for every subject whether it's Accounts, Maths, Business Studies, Economics, Science, etc. We do not only provide education till Class 12 but also help students in admissions for JBT, B.Ed, M.Ed, B.Pharma, B.Com, etc.
